     Chicken Marsala

INGREDIENTS

  • 1lb of chicken
  • ¼ lb mushrooms
  • 6 tbls butter
  • ⅓ cup flour
  • ⅓ cup grated cheese
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 2 tbls crushed garlic
  • ½ cup beef stock consomme
  • ½ cup marsala wine
  • salt/ pepper to taste

STEP ONE: Saute mushrooms in 2 tbls butter about 10 minutes, set aside.
STEP TWO: Pound meat with meat tenderizer.
STEP THREE: Combine flour, cheese and seasoning, dredge meat into mixture,
pressing mixture into meat with hands.
STEP FOUR: In large skillet, heat remaining butter and oil, add garlic then meat
browning lightly on both sides for 3 minutes.
STEP FIVE: Pour in stock and wine, bring to a boil, cover and simmer 25 minutes.
STEP SIX: Add mushroom and cook 5 minutes longer.

Serve over pasta, rice, or potatoes.
